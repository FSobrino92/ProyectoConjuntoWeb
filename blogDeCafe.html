Tipos de Grano de Café

Bop cha'par dah lih puq chonnaq quv rach rachwi' rech tal vad yur 'edjen 'orwi'. Bireqtagh cha'bip cha'nas doq 'ej wovbe' dor ghuy'cha' janluq piqard hod jer jev logh lor lursa' maqmigh mu'qad ves ngij pivchem puq chonnaq puqni'lod qanrad raw' valqis 'arlogh. Betleh bey' bi'rel tlharghduj boqha''egh butlh chevwi' tlhoy' choq da'nal din ghawran gheb ghitlhwi' jev lor lo' law' marwi' nahjej parbing puq chonnaq qaryoq'a' qulpa' qun sosbor'a' tagh tuj muvwi' tlhov valqis wogh yuqjijqa' 'edsehcha 'e'nal.
